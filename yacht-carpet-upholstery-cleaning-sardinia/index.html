Onboard yacht interiors

Marine textile care for carpets, upholstery, mattresses and charter-ready interiors

The service is designed for yachts and high-end interiors that require careful cleaning, fast response and discreet onboard work. It is suitable for captains, chief stewardesses, yacht managers, charter managers and private owners who need carpets, upholstery and mattresses cleaned before guest arrival or after heavy onboard use.

Yacht carpet cleaning

Cleaning for saloon carpets, cabin carpets, runners, fitted carpets and yacht moquette affected by foot traffic, humidity, spills, odours and everyday onboard use.

Yacht upholstery cleaning

Careful cleaning for sofas, cushions, interior seating, fabric panels and guest areas where textile appearance matters before a charter, owner visit or guest boarding.

Mattress sanitisation

Mattress cleaning and sanitisation for guest cabins, owner cabins and crew areas, useful for periodic hygiene, odours, humidity-related issues and charter preparation.

Stain & odour treatment

First assessment and targeted treatment for wine stains, spills, odours and mould-related textile issues, depending on fabric type, age of the stain and onboard conditions.
